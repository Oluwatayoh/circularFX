Waste Collection and
Recycling

In line with the vision of EcoCircleAfrica which is to create a marketplace for the exchange of recyclable materials and commodities, EcoCircleAfrica will aggregate the price of recyclable materials based on the natural market demand and supply by sourcing price data from franchisees and vendors serving as the market price. Data of the daily rates are published on the EcoCircleAfrica platform showing local market price and the foreign market price of each country thereby allowing subscribers to make a comparative analysis on their best bet.

Glass

Glass is an inorganic solid material that is usually transparent or translucent as well as hard, brittle, and impervious to the natural elements.

Ferrous Metals

Ferrous metals refer to any metal that contains iron. They are favoured for their tensile strength and durability, so are often utilised in housing construction, large-scale piping and industrial containers.
