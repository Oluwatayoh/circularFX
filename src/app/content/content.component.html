Waste Collection and
Recycling

In line with the vision of Circular FX which is to create a marketplace for the exchange of recyclable materials and commodities, Circular FX will aggregate the price of recyclable materials based on the natural market demand and supply by sourcing price data from franchisees and vendors serving as the market price. Data of the daily rates are published on the Circular FX platform showing local market price and the foreign market price of each country thereby allowing subscribers to make a comparative analysis on their best bet.
